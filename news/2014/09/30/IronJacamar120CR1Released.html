IronJacamar 1.2.0.CR1 released

September 30th, 2014

I'm proud to announce the IronJacamar 1.2.0.CR1 release. Full release notes are here .

Candidate for Release

Reaching Candidate for Release is an important milestone for the IronJacamar community. The journey towards our 1.2 release has been longer than expected, but saw quite a number of new features that hopefully will serve the Java EE Connector Architecture community in their work.

The feedback we have received has really benefitted the release, so a huge thanks for that ! Of course our forums are open for additional feedback, so feel free to speak up on any issues you may find, or just getting help on your JCA development tasks.

Changes

This release was relative quiet, as we went through the existing functionality to look for missing parts. There were a couple of new features that stands out,

  • [JBJCA-1208] Eclipse plugin updates for IronJacamar 1.2
  • [JBJCA-1220] Enhancements of our graceful shutdown functionality
  • [JBJCA-1214] XAResource statistics for each pool
  • [JBJCA-1211] Support for JDK 8

The release contains some bug fixes as well.

The Road Ahead

Hopefully testing will go smooth over the next couple of weeks, and hence we will see a Final release some time during October. Please, report anything you may find, as the next development series will be 2.0, and a completely new journey :)

Enjoy !

On behalf of the IronJacamar community,
    Jesper
